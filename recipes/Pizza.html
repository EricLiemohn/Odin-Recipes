Homemade Pizza

Pizza Recipe

Learn how to make pizza at home! This is the only guide you’ll ever need for making homemade pizza in the oven

There are just a few secrets and tips for making pizza at home with store bought dough. This recipe is so simple you can make pizza at home without a pizza stone or a rolling pin. You don’t even need to make homemade pizza dough! All you need is ~20 minutes, a sheet pan, pre-made store bought dough, and a desire to eat delicious homemade pizza.

Ingredients

  • Store Bought Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese Blend (Mozzerella and cheddar)
  • Toppings of choice

Steps

  1. Spread Dough
  2. Apply Sauce
  3. Apply Cheese
  4. Apply toppings
  5. Bake in oven at 4000 degrees for 30 seconds
  6. Let rest for 10 mins and serve
